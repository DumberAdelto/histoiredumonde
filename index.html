Tir Rapide

(rapid fire)






The soviets wanted to be in space before the americans to prove their technological and intellectual superiority in the Cold war.
- Sputnik 1 was the first artificial satellite in space.

- Launched by the Soviet Union.
Another achivement by the Soviet Union. They led the space race at first, but only because of the amount of money they were putting in it.
- Soviet Cosmonaut Yuri Gagarin was the first in space.

Once America invested some money as well, they were the ones in the lead.
In the highest point of the space race, the US were vying to achive some big milestones in space history. Thats where the US came in — with Apollo 8. Their 2nd manned mission, they strove to reach the moon. And reach the moon they did.
- Apollo 8 was the first manned human spaceflight to reach the moon.

They became the first human spaceflight to reach the moon. They orbitted the moon 10 times before touching down back on earth.
Marking the end of the space race, Apollo 11 made a historical milestone, containing the first 2 people to walk on the moon. Launched on July 16, 1969, Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin landed the Lunar Module Eagle on July 20, 1969. They brought back 21.5kg of lunar material.
- Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldren, members of the Apollo 11 misson were the first people to walk on the moon.

Michael Collins was the pilot, and flew the Command module Columbia in lunar orbit. They were on the lunar surface for 21 hours before lifting off to rejoin Columbia.
